Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026  ·  Effective: February 23, 2026

🛡️ Guardian Ring is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the data necessary to keep you safe. We never sell your personal information.

1. Who We Are

Guardian Ring ("we", "us", or "our") is a women's safety Android application developed by Lulu Fathimma. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at lufxi2005@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our safety services:

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location is collected when you trigger an SOS alert or enable live location sharing. This data is sent to your designated emergency contacts.
  • Phone Numbers: Emergency contact phone numbers you manually enter in the app. These are stored locally on your device.
  • Sensor Data: Accelerometer data (shake detection) and microphone input (voice command detection) are processed locally on-device to detect SOS triggers. This data is not stored or transmitted.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (OS version, device model) may be collected for app performance and crash reporting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send emergency SOS alerts and your GPS location to your chosen emergency contacts
  • To enable live family location tracking features
  • To detect shake and voice triggers for hands-free SOS activation
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • Emergency Contacts: Your location and distress message are shared with emergency contacts you have pre-selected in the app.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services (such as Google Maps for location) that have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Permissions We Request

Guardian Ring requires the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: To share your GPS coordinates during an SOS alert.
  • SEND_SMS: To send emergency SMS messages to your contacts.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: For voice command detection to trigger SOS silently.
  • READ_CONTACTS: To let you select emergency contacts from your phone book.
  • CALL_PHONE: To initiate emergency calls.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restart background safety monitoring after device reboot.
  • VIBRATE / FLASHLIGHT: For the siren and flashlight alert features.

6. Data Storage & Security

Most data (emergency contacts, settings) is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to external servers. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect any data that is transmitted. Location data sent during SOS is transmitted over secure connections.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary. Emergency contact details are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time within the app settings. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at lufxi2005@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Guardian Ring is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps / Google Location Services
  • Firebase (Google) for crash analytics

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
